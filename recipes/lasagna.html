Cheese Lasagna

Description

This cheese lasagna recipe is an easy vegetarian lasagna with ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheeses.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Ricotta
  • 4 eggs
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Mix Ricotta and Eggs
  2. Make Layers using the noodles and the cheese mix
  3. Bake for 50 Min
